MR PRICE

Mr Price Group Limited, is a publicly traded retail company based in South Africa. Founded in 1985, Mr Price opened in 1987 after the owners bought a controlling interest in John Orrs Holdings, whose trading divisions at the time were The Hub and Miladys. Currently their trading divisions are Mr Price, Miladys, Mr Price Sport, Mr Price Home and Sheet Street

Mr Price Foundation is a Non-Profit Benefit Organization. The organization empowers young South Africans through its education and skills development programmers through Mr Price Foundation Schools and Jump Start.
