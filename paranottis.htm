PARANOTTIS

The Panarottis menu has been serving up fantastic food to South Africans since 1990. They’ve grown big time, and now have over 100 restaurants – most of them in South Africa, with a few across the Indian Ocean in Australia. They even have some in the Canal Walk mall. One of the reasons they’ve grown so steadily is in their dough.
